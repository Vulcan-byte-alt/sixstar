Cruise Overview

Embark on an unforgettable 15-night transatlantic voyage from the vibrant streets of Barcelona to the sun-soaked shores of Miami. This epic journey crosses the Atlantic Ocean, visiting stunning ports in Spain, Portugal, Madeira, Bermuda, and Florida.

Aboard the award-winning Regent Seven Seas Explorer, you'll experience the pinnacle of luxury cruising with all-inclusive amenities, gourmet dining, and personalized service that exceeds every expectation.

Cruise Highlights

  • Transatlantic crossing from Barcelona to Miami visiting Spain, Madeira, and Bermuda
  • All-inclusive dining with unlimited specialty restaurants
  • Complimentary shore excursions in every port of call
  • Premium beverages including fine wines and champagne
  • Butler service in all suites for the ultimate luxury experience

Day by Day

Your Itinerary

Discover the carefully planned journey that awaits you on this epic transatlantic voyage

1

Barcelona, Spain

Embarkation Day - Depart 7:00 PM

Begin your transatlantic adventure in the vibrant city of Barcelona. The infinite variety of street life, the nooks and crannies of the medieval Barri Gtic, the ceramic tile and stained glass of Art Nouveau facades, Gaud's masterpiece La Sagrada FamliaBarcelona will capture your full attention. Explore the Gothic Quarter, stroll down La Rambla, and savor tapas before embarking on your epic ocean crossing.

2

Cruising the Mediterranean Sea

Day at Sea

Relax and enjoy a full day cruising through the beautiful Mediterranean Sea. Take advantage of the ship's world-class spa, attend enrichment lectures, or simply unwind on deck with a cocktail as you prepare for your Spanish coast arrival.

3

Mlaga, Spain

8:00 AM - 6:00 PM

As you sail into Mlaga, notice its idyllic setting on the famous Costa del Sol. This provincial capital sits between charming fishing villages to the east and vibrant coastal cities to the west. The Penibtica Mountains provide a spectacular backdrop, sheltering the province from cold winds and creating a therapeutic climate. Mlaga is the gateway to Andalusia's enchanting historic villages and the birthplace of Picasso.

Complimentary Shore Excursion: Picasso Museum & Andalusian Old Town Walking Tour
4

Sailing the Atlantic Ocean

Day at Sea

Enjoy a relaxing day at sea as we cross into the Atlantic Ocean. Take advantage of the ship's world-class amenities, from the Canyon Ranch SpaClub to enrichment lectures and cooking demonstrations. Unwind by the pool or indulge in afternoon tea as you watch the ocean horizons.

5

Funchal, Madeira

8:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Formed by a volcanic eruption, Madeira lies in the Gulf Stream, about 500 miles due west of Casablanca. This beautiful Portuguese island is known for its dramatic landscapes, lush gardens, and fortified wine. Explore the colorful capital of Funchal with its charming old town, vibrant markets, and stunning botanical gardens perched on hillsides offering breathtaking ocean views.

Complimentary Shore Excursion: Monte Palace Tropical Garden & Historic Funchal Walking Tour
6-10

Transatlantic Crossing

5 Days at Sea - Atlantic Ocean

Experience the romance and tranquility of a true transatlantic crossing. These five days at sea offer the ultimate luxury cruise experience with endless opportunities to relax, rejuvenate, and enjoy world-class entertainment and dining.

Onboard Activities:

  • Canyon Ranch SpaClub
  • Specialty Dining
  • Live Entertainment
  • Enrichment Lectures
  • Pool & Hot Tubs
  • Observation Lounge
11-12

Hamilton, Bermuda

2 Days - 8:00 AM - 10:00 PM each day

Spend two glorious days in Bermuda's charming capital. With its colorful pastel buildings, pink sand beaches, and crystal-clear turquoise waters, Hamilton offers a perfect blend of British colonial charm and island paradise. Explore the historic Royal Naval Dockyard, relax on world-famous Horseshoe Bay Beach, or discover the underwater wonders while snorkeling in the coral reefs.

Day 11 Excursion: Crystal Caves & St. George's UNESCO World Heritage Town
Day 12 Excursion: Horseshoe Bay Beach Experience & Gibbs Hill Lighthouse
13-14

Sailing the Atlantic Ocean

2 Days at Sea

As we approach the final leg of our transatlantic journey, enjoy two more days of pure luxury at sea. Reflect on your incredible voyage, savor farewell dinners in specialty restaurants, and prepare for your arrival in vibrant Miami.

15

Miami, Florida

Disembarkation - Arrive 7:00 AM

Your unforgettable transatlantic voyage concludes in the sun-drenched Magic City. Disembark in Miami with memories of an epic ocean crossing, stunning ports of call, and the incomparable luxury of Regent Seven Seas Cruises. Consider extending your stay to explore Miami's vibrant Art Deco District, world-class shopping, and beautiful beaches.
